# Task 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) - Report

## Dataset Used: Titanic Dataset

### Objective:
To explore and analyze the Titanic dataset and derive patterns related to passenger survival.

### Key Insights:
- Most passengers did not survive.
- Females had a higher survival rate compared to males.
- Passengers from higher classes (Pclass 1) had better chances of survival.
- Missing values are present in "Age", "Cabin", and "Embarked".
- Age distribution shows most passengers were in the 20-40 age range.

### Libraries Used:
- pandas
- seaborn
- matplotlib
